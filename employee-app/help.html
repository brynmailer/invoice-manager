Work Sessions

Your work sessions are displayed as a list of collapsibles running down your screen with the date and time of the work sessions start and finish. You can view the details of a work session by tapping on the down arrow to the far right of the work session in question.

The information displayed in this expanded work session view includes:

  • • The project that the work session belongs to
  • • The description of the work session

By tapping to the pencil icon you can open the edit work session screen, allowing you to change a work sessions details. You can also tap on the bin icon to delete the work session. To create a new work session, tap the round button at the bottom right of your screen, this will bring up a form that allows you to enter the details for a new work session.

Settings

By tapping the three bars at the top left of your screen you can open the side navigation menu, from which you can navigate to the settings page. This page allows you to alternate between the two colour themes of the the application.
